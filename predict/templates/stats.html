Total Covid Cases/per million Cases

These are the images of Total Covid Cases and Total Covid Cases per Million population in India and some other Countries.

Total Covid Deaths/per million Deaths

These are the images of Total Covid Deaths and Total Covid Deaths per Million population in India and some other Countries.
In these Images we can see that the absolute number of deaths are higher in India but the deaths per million cases are relatively low.



Total Vaccination/population percentage vaccination

These are the Stats of Vaccination of people in India and percentage of population got vaccinated.
In these Images we can see that the absolute number of Vaccination are higher in India but the Vaccination of percentage of population is relatively low.
